Connect—it

Over the past 20 years, global efforts to combat climate change have expanded beyond the multilateral, state-led climate governance. Numerous cross-border initiatives comprising both state and non-state actors each contribute to solve these global problems. Navigating this complexity of contemporary climate governance requires new ways of thinking. Connect-it has been designed to enhance your understanding of global climate governance by using visual and interactive tools. Zoom, scroll, and click your way through our data-set to get a better grip on who is doing what and with whom.

Research: Flávia Dias Guerra, Oscar Widerberg, Marija Isailovic, Philipp Pattberg
Design and programming: Template

How Connect—it works

There are six different ways to navigate the Connect-it data.
If you simply wish to download the full data-set click here.

Geography displays the headquarters of each institution

Governance Triangle situates institutions depending on their constituents

Disc provides a flat view with multiple options for analysis

List is an alternative to ‘disc’

Description briefly describes each institution

Network links institutions by shared members
